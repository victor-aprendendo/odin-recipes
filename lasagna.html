Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a Italian dish which conquered all kitchens around the world in the 50s.

It can be served in any occasion. From kids birthday parties to funerals.

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Selery
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  1. Cook the lasagna sheets.
  2. In a fry pan, add olive oil, then cook chopped onion, carrot and selery.
  3. Add minced meat and black pepper.
  4. Add tomato sauce, then mix to have a bolognese sauce.
  5. In a casserole pan, alternate between the bolognese sauce the lasagna sheets and the cheese
  6. Finalize with cheese toppings.
  7. Put in the oven until the cheese on top looks sexy.
  8. Wait to not burn your soul. Buon appetito!
